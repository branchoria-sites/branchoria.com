Credits & Resources

This page collects acknowledgements for reusable maps, icons, emoji graphics, and other open-source resources used across Branchoria.com and related Branchoria properties.

Maps

Some Branchoria sites use map base artwork from SimpleMaps World SVG Map. The map may be adapted for styling, accessibility, linking, or responsive display. SimpleMaps lists the SVG world map as free for commercial and personal use, with attribution appreciated.

Icons

Branchoria interfaces and generated visual assets may use icons from Lucide. Lucide is released under the ISC License, with some icons derived from the Feather project under the MIT License.

Emoji Graphics

Where Branchoria-generated visual assets use bundled emoji artwork rather than browser-native emoji rendering, those assets may include open-source emoji graphics such as Twemoji and Fluent Emoji. Twemoji graphics are licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International, and Fluent Emoji is licensed by Microsoft under the MIT License.

Page Sources

Individual research pages may cite their own article, book, data, image, video, archive, or reference sources close to the relevant material. Those page-level citations are separate from these reusable design and publishing-tool credits.

Updates

Branchoria sites and publishing tools change over time. This page may be updated as new reusable resources, open-source libraries, or attribution requirements are added to the network.
